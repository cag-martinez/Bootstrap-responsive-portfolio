About me

My name is Christian, I am a former Sous Chef at the Peninsula Club with 7 plus years of experience in the field. Through my years as a chef I have had the experience to help the non-profits and create great memories to remember for famous guest and the surrounding community. It has ben a great journey although it is now time to change the page and start a new chapter, which is why I chose to sign up to this bootcamp. I enjoy learning new things constantly and asking "why" and the curiosity of why website work the way they do and how they all link to one another is the reason I decided to learn web development and make a career change. Aside from learning new things, I also enjoy being outdoors. During the warm Summer days I like spending hours on the lake kayaking, or hiking mountain trails and every so often I like to play disc-golf.
